About Me



My name is Vinnie Tranquillo. I am 25 years old. I am oringally from Ebensburg, PA, but I currently reside in Pittsburgh, PA. I am a Systems Analyst at PNC Bank.

When I am not doing that, I am probably watching some sort of sporting event. I love sports so much! The Pittsburgh Steelers are hands down my favorite professional team across all sports, but I am also a big supporter of the other two teams here in Pittsburgh.

My love for sports inspired me to become an official, and this upcoming football season, I will be working as an NCAA Football Official.
